Who we are

A Sub Above — in everything we do

Legend Subs is a proud multi-unit Jersey Mike's franchise operating across Northern New England. Founded on the belief that serving others is the highest calling, we operate 8 locations across Massachusetts and New Hampshire with a vision of growing to 20+ thriving stores.

Legend Subs is built around five founding values: Humility, Commitment, Kindness, Loyalty, and Purpose. These aren't posters on a wall — they're how we hire, train, recognize, and promote our people every single day.

We follow Jersey Mike's 7 Principles of Success, which guide everything from how we keep our stores to how we show up for our customers. When you join Legend Subs, you join a culture rooted in the betterment of customers, employees, and community.

Career Growth

From Crew to Operations Director

Every Legend starts somewhere. Our career path is clearly defined, performance-driven, and built to take you as far as you're willing to go.

CM
Crew Member → Shift Leader 0–2 Months

Master core stations, customer service, and opening/closing procedures. Complete Phase 1 and 3 certifications while learning to lead shifts and support managers.

SL
Shift Leader → Manager in Training 2–6 Months

Take ownership of store operations, staffing, and training. Drive catering and community growth while learning P&L fundamentals. Develop confidence and leadership readiness for full store management.

MIT
Manager in Training → General Manager 3–8 Months

Oversee all store operations — sales, people, and guest experience. Hire and train future leaders, build community partnerships, and live out the 7 Principles in daily leadership.

GM
General Manager → District Manager 2+ Years

Lead multiple stores, develop GMs and MITs, and expand catering and community initiatives. Work with the executive team to support regional growth and consistency.

DM
District Manager → Director of Operations 3+ Years

Provide strategic leadership across markets, open new stores, and mentor the next generation of leaders. Help drive our vision toward 20+ thriving stores across Northern New England.

Giving back

A culture rooted in community

"At Legend Subs you are part of a culture that is rooted in philanthropy and the betterment of its customers, employees, and community."

Jersey Mike's was founded on the idea that a business exists to serve — not just sandwiches, but people. Every year, Jersey Mike's holds a Month of Giving, donating 100% of a single day's sales to local charities. Our franchise proudly participates and encourages every team member to see themselves as a part of something bigger.

When you work at Legend Subs, you're part of a team that shows up for its neighbors the same way it shows up for its customers — with generosity, heart, and purpose.

🍞

Jersey Mike's Month of Giving
